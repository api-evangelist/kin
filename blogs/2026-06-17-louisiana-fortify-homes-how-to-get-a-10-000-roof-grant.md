The Louisiana Fortify Homes Program provides grants up to $10,000 to eligible homeowners who upgrade their roof to meet the FORTIFIED standard designed to withstand hurricane-force winds. Beyond the grant, a FORTIFIED designation can unlock discounted home insurance rates and increase property value in hurricane-prone areas.
