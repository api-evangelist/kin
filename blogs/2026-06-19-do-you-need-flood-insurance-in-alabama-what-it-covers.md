Standard Alabama homeowners insurance does not cover flood damage, so residents must add a flood endorsement, buy a separate private policy, or enroll through the National Flood Insurance Program (NFIP). The article covers coverage options, costs of roughly $825/year median, and prevention strategies for properties facing heavy rainfall and hurricane risks.
