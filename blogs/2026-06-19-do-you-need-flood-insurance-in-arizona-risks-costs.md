Standard homeowners insurance in Arizona excludes flood damage, so homeowners need a separate flood policy or endorsement. The article explains why flooding is a significant risk despite Arizona's dry climate, compares private and NFIP flood insurance options at an average cost around $760/year, and outlines prevention strategies.
